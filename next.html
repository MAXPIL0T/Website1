Senior 2020 Reflection

By Maximilian Kuechen

UMass Amherst

I am going to attend UMass Amherst next year.

After starting the college search in spring of 2019 I knew that I wanted to go to a school that was bigger than Newton North, had good academics, and was close to home while not being directly in my backyard.

The college application process was frustrating and long, but it all turned out well in the end!

Education

I don't know what the future hold yet.

I am currently on the exploratory track for Education, but am still unsure of what exactly I want to do. I am considering Computer Science, Secondary Education, and History. I guess I will have to decide a some point, but I still have time.

I am sure that I am well prepared for my UMass education because of my NNHS education.
