Senior 2020 Reflection

By Maximilian Kuechen

9th Grade

Welcome to 9th grade, freshman year, and the CRINGE is real.

Just four years ago I was the most socially awkward in my life, and had a serious superiority complex. Basically all the qualities a freshman has. Every year the freshmen seem to get worse, but maybe we are just maturing and are realizing the sheer stupidity that goes on inside a freshman's head.

No freshman me will realize this then, but looking back it is hard to comprehend that I am the same person now as four years ago, but that shows growth right???

10th Grade

Ah yes, sophomore year... Also known as the most forgettable year.

Seriously, life was pretty great, but nothing really happened. College seemed so far off and wasn't a worry on my mind yet. My classes were still easy for the most part and things were chill.

In hindsight, I should have gotten all A's and gotten my GPA off to a good start, but you know what they say, HINDSIGHT IS 2020!!!

I'll see myself out...

11th Grade

Junior year, not bad, pretty good actually!

Yeah, I had great classes, got to know new people and had a good time. Unfortunately my grades didn't have as much fun in 11th grade as I did. Also it was a shock to find out that things actually mattered now and that I would have to stress about college soon.

Overall I should have been more responsible with my work, but still managed to keep my grades up above a B in most classes, although there was no reason to do bad in Bio or French other than my own laziness.

12th Grade

Senior year, the big finish, the end. It's here.

Somehow I got the best grades since 7th grade in the year with the hardest classed since 7th grade. I don't know why, but I think I finally realized that I would have to work hard to have a chance of getting into a good school. I got a 1520 on the SAT which helped my motivation a lot.

This year I was also able to relieve all my stress in Culinary and have a clearer head as a result.

We all know what happened next...
