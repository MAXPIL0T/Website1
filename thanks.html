Thank You Teachers!

Over the years at Newton North I have had many teachers.

The overwhelming majority of those teachers have been nothing short of amazing. The teachers at this school really care about their students, and love their jobs. I am so thankful to have had the opportunity to know so many of them.

I want to thank all the teachers that I have had for their enthusiasm and their dedication to their jobs and their students. Below is a list of all the teaches, in order of blocks, I have had during my time at north. To all of you,

Thank you.
